Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · aliamazn98@gmail.com

🔒 SnipTranslate does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Text you translate is sent directly to translation APIs and never stored on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

  • Text for translation: Sent directly to third-party APIs. Never stored by us.
  • Images for OCR: Sent to OCR.space API. Never stored by us.
  • Usage count: Stored locally in your browser to enforce the free tier daily limit.
  • Language preferences: Stored locally in your browser for convenience.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, or personal identifiers
  • Your browsing history or visited websites
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • We do not sell or share data with advertisers

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Translate API: Used for translation. Subject to Google Privacy Policy.
  • MyMemory API: Fallback translation. Subject to MyMemory Privacy Policy.
  • OCR.space API: Image text recognition (Pro). Subject to OCR.space Privacy Policy.
  • Paddle: Payment processor. Subject to Paddle Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

All preferences and usage data are stored locally on your device using Chrome built-in storage APIs. This data does not leave your browser except as described above.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

6. Contact

For questions, contact us at aliamazn98@gmail.com.
