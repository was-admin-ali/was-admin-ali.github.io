Frequently asked questions

How do I activate my Pro license?
After payment, you'll receive a license key by email. Open the SnipTranslate popup, find the "Enter License Key" field, paste your key and click Activate. Pro features unlock instantly.
Can I use the extension on multiple devices?
Yes. Your license key can be used on any Chrome browser you use. Simply paste the same key on each device.
What happens when my subscription ends?
Your extension reverts to the free tier (20 translations/day, text only). Your settings and history are preserved.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes — we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. See our Refund Policy for details.
Is the Lifetime plan really forever?
Yes. One payment, permanent Pro access, all future updates included. No recurring charges ever.
